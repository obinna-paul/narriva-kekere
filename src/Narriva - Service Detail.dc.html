Editorial

The work of making a good manuscript into the best version of itself.

photo · editor marking up a manuscript

Editing isn't proofreading. A developmental edit looks at the whole shape of a book — what it's trying to do, where it loses the reader, where the real story is hiding two chapters in. A line edit goes sentence by sentence for rhythm, clarity, and voice. Most manuscripts need some of both, and we'll tell you honestly which.
