Adaeze Okonkwo

Writes literary fiction about Lagos's coastal underworld.

Adaeze Okonkwo was born in Badagry and raised between the salt flats and the city. She spent a decade as a journalist — covering shipping, smuggling, and the long memory of the coast — before turning to fiction.

Her debut novel, The Salt Roads of Badagry, draws on the world she grew up in: a place where a family's debts are measured in salt and silence. She is currently at work on her second novel.
